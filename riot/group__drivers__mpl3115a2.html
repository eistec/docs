Detailed Description

Driver for the Freescale MPL3115A2 pressure sensor.

The driver will initialize the sensor for pressure measurement. The conversion duration depends on oversample ratio. After initialization and set activ the sensor will make measurements at periodic times. The oversample ratio can be determined by sensor initialization.
